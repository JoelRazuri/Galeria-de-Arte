Since its creation, the Gallery has set several objectives that it has been fulfilling and that allows it to differentiate itself from traditional Galleries:

- In the first place, and in line with the activity as a gallery owner, the objective is to support its artists by accompanying them in different ways; such as the realization of samples, books and other ways for recognition.

- A second objective, and perhaps one of the most comforting aspects for a gallery owner, is to accompany those people who come to our space interested in art investment, to assist them in trying to help transform that investor into an art collector.

This metamorphosis is, in essence, the difference between the art market and other ordinary markets; because to become a collector you have to put something more than money and that can be summed up in one word: PASSION
